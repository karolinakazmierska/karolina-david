How to get there?

Otrębusy is located 30min drive away from Warsaw city center (~20km) / 1hr 45min drive away from Łódź city center (~120km). Please consider leaving a bit earlier to avoid heavy traffic as Wednesday is the first day of a long weekend in Poland (Boże Ciało). There is plenty of space to park a car both nearby the Church and in front of Pałacyk Otrębusy.

Otrębusy has a direct railway link with Warsaw city center. Take a WKD train from Warszawa Śródmieście WKD station and get off at Otrębusy WKD station. The church is located 11min walk from the station. Check the itinerary on Jak Dojade

A ride from Warsaw takes approximately 30 min and should cost you around 40-60 PLN.
