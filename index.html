Wednesday

4:30 pm

Wedding ceremony:

Church of St Mary, Mother of the Church, 14 Sadowa Street, Otrebusy, Poland

Wedding reception:

Pałacyk Otrebusy, 2 Warszawska Street, Otrebusy

How to get there?

From Warsaw city center: 20km (~30min)
From Łódź city center: 120km (~1h45min).
Please consider leaving a bit earlier to avoid heavy traffic as Wednesday is the first day of a long weekend in Poland (Boże Ciało). Parking space available both nearby the Church and in front of Pałacyk Otrębusy.

Otrębusy has a direct railway link with Warsaw city center. Take a WKD train from Warszawa Śródmieście WKD station and get off at Otrębusy WKD station. The church is located 11min walk from the station. Check the itinerary on Jak Dojade

A ride from Warsaw takes approximately 30 min and should cost you around 40-60 PLN.
